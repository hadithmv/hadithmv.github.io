While translating Umdatul Ahkam (288), I encountered the Hadith of Wasiyya from Bukhari (2738): "It is not befitting for a Muslim who has anything concerning which a will should be made, to spend two nights without having a written will with him." I referred to the wording of how Wasiyya should be written from Sunan ad Darimi (3092) and Baihaqi (12807).
