  • Things that are forbidden in the state of Ihram: (For both MEN and WOMEN:) 1. Removing anything from your skin, hair or nails. 2. Letting any perfume or scent touch your body or clothes. 3. Having intercourse and touching with desire. 4. Hunting game animals. 5. Entering into a marriage contract, or making a marriage contract for someone else. (For MEN only:) 1. Wearing sewn clothes (stitched to fit the body), like shirts and pants. 2. Covering the head. 3. Wearing footwear that covers the heel, ankle and top of the foot. (For WOMEN only:) 1. Wearing a Niqab (a covering veil that fits the face) and wearing gloves.
