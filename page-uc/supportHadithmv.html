1. Donation

Help Spread the Sunnah - Your Support Makes a Difference

The Hadithmv Project is a unique initiative dedicated to making the authentic teachings of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ and his righteous followers freely accessible to the Maldivian community in their native language, Dhivehi. By providing free, high-quality translations of Hadith, Quran, and Islamic texts, we aim to empower individuals with knowledge and strengthen their connection to the Sunnah. Your support can help us continue this vital work and expand our project to benefit people even more. By donating, you become part of this effort to preserve and share knowledge.

Why Support Hadithmv?

  • Accessibility for All: Hadithmv is committed to breaking barriers of language, wealth, and location. Your donation ensures that every Maldivian, regardless of their circumstances, can access authentic Islamic knowledge for free.
  • Sadaqah Jariyah: By supporting Hadithmv, you contribute to a continuous act of charity. Every person who benefits from the resources we provide becomes a source of ongoing reward for you.
  • Empowering the Ummah: Many of the challenges faced by our society stem from a lack of understanding of the Sunnah. Your donation helps us educate and inspire individuals, families, and communities to live by the teachings of Islam.
  • Preserving Knowledge: Hadithmv is building a comprehensive database of Dhivehi translations of Hadith, Quran, and Islamic texts. Your contribution helps us preserve and share this knowledge for generations to come.

How Your Donation Helps

Every contribution, no matter how small, becomes a Sadaqah Jariyah - an ongoing charity - that will continue to benefit both the donor and the community, and when someone learns from our platform, you get to share in the reward of their knowledge, in Sha Allah. Your contribution helps us to:

  • Keep all content free and accessible for everyone.
  • Expand our library with more translations of Hadith, Quranic tafsirs, and Islamic texts.
  • Maintain high standards of accuracy and focus on authentic, reliable sources.
  • Develop and improve our free platforms (Web, Android, Windows) and enhance features and user experience.
  • Enable our team of translators, developers, and scholars to focus on content development rather than fundraising.
  • Ensure sustainable growth of the platform.

Hadithmv’s Impact So Far

  • ✅ 10+ Books of Hadith and Athar translated into Dhivehi (with over 8,000 entries.)
  • ✅ 6+ Quran Translations and Tafsirs, to help readers understand the Quran in their language.
  • ✅ 10+ Books on Aqida and Usooluddeen to strengthen faith and understanding of Islamic principles.
  • ✅ 5 Dictionaries to aid in learning and research (with over 100,000 entries.)

Be Part of a Lasting Legacy

The Hadithmv Project is more than just a platform – it’s a movement to bring the light of the Sunnah to every corner of the Maldives. With your help, we can continue to provide free, accessible, and authentic Islamic knowledge to everyone. Join us in this noble cause. Your donation will be an ongoing Sadaqah Jariyah, benefiting you in this life and the next, in Sha Allah.

މި މަޝްރޫއަށް އެހީވެދިނުމަށް މިތަނަށް ފިތާލައްވައިގެން އަރަބި ނައްސުތަކެއް ތަރުޖަމާ ކޮށްލަދެއްވާ.

Instructions:

• Translate only if you know Arabic, and do not make Ta’weel of Islamic texts.

• To translate anonymously, open the link above in incognito mode.

• You don’t have to write “Sahabi rivaa kurevvi, rasoolaa hadith kurevvi”, we will write those and make them consistent.

• Likewise, you don’t have to translate the Takhrij. eg: “Rivaa kurevvee Bukhari”.

• You can make use of the Hadithmv Text Editor to write faster and use shortcuts.

• You can make use of Sunnah.com to check the English translation. Note that they have mistakes.

• You can make use of Dorar.net to check the Arabic explanation.

• Try to write at least 1 hadith a week at minimum. If you are able, feel free to try more than that.
